Quesadilla

Yummy quesadilla filled with cheese and ham.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tortillas
  • Shredded cheese
  • Shredded ham

Directions

  1. Put first tortilla on cold pan
  2. Spread shredded cheese and ham on tortilla
  3. Put second tortilla on top
  4. Heat pan over medium-high heat
  5. Flip quesadilla when bottom is toasty.
