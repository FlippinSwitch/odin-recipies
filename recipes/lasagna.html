Lasagna

A delicious lasagna. Yummy in my tummy then out my bummy.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Lasagna meat sauce
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  1. Preheat oven to 200c
  2. In a cassarole dish, put a layer of meat sauce
  3. Place noodle sheets over sauce
  4. Layer bechamel sauce over noodle sheets
  5. Repeat layers, sauce,noodle, bechamel two more times
  6. On top layer of bechamel place shredded/cubed mozzarella
